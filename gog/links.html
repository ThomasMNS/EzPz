Guilds of Gods Links

Written by MilkN2Sugars. Last updated 25 September 2019.

Guides and walkthroughs

  • GoGzone: An amazing fansite by Inspiria with tables for every skill and annotated world and dungeon maps.
  • Guilds of Gods Wiki: The Fandom wiki for Guilds of Gods.
  • Market list by Marianne: Data on a variety of items including monster drops, thieving loot and shop buy and sell prices.
  • Underground AFK XP guide by Volrtaw: Locations of the best AFK training spots.
  • Bestiary by Deathclaw: Monster levels and drops.
  • XP and max hit calculations: XP required for each level, and hit chance, max hit and min hit based on your and your opponents stats.
  • Experience calculator by Inspiria: Calculates how many actions you need to carry out to reach a desired level.
  • Skill training tips by Sandwich: Practical skill training tips from teh first maxed player
